NP Membership Benefits

Cost: No annual fee

  • Community networking opportunities
  • Basic listing in the online member directory
  • Access to chamber events at a discounted rate
  • Eligibility for chamber grants (when available)

Bronze Membership Benefits

Cost: $100/year

  • All NP benefits
  • Standard listing with logo in the online member directory
  • Discounted rates for workshops and training sessions
  • Opportunity to participate in member-exclusive promotions

Silver Membership Benefits

Cost: $250/year

  • All Bronze benefits
  • Enhanced directory listing with extended description
  • Priority invitations to special events and luncheons
  • Opportunity for "Member Spotlight" feature on social media
  • Small business advisory services access

Gold Membership Benefits

Cost: $500/year

  • All Silver benefits
  • Premium placement in the online member directory
  • Exclusive invitations to leadership roundtables and VIP events
  • Dedicated chamber liaison for business support
  • Opportunity for prominent advertising on the chamber website homepage
  • Complimentary attendance at select chamber events
